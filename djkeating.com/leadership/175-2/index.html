Nina Keating Fisher

General Counsel

As General Counsel, Nina Keating Fisher is responsible for the creation and review of contracts, general litigation, negotiation and representation on behalf of the firm, as well as corporate recording and documentation. She received her undergraduate degree summa cum laude from Chestnut Hill College, and her law degree, cum laude, from Villanova Law School, where she served as an editor of the Villanova Law Review.
